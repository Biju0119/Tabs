Beauty Made Easy

Welcome to the McLean Clinic website!

I am a plastic surgeon, both Canadian and American Board certified, specializing exclusively in cosmetic surgery. My many years of experience and training have helped the people of Mississauga, Toronto and the GTA to achieve the look they desire. The most common procedures performed in our accredited private surgical facility are face lift, brow lift and eye lid surgery, breast augmentation, tummy tuck, and liposuction.

Our nurse injector and medical aesthetician do non-surgical procedures including fillers such as Juvederm and Restylane, microdermabrasion, peels, laser treatments, and skin care. Our unique techniques live up to our motto: "Beauty Made Easy...
